# ADR-0005: Use Topic Relationships For V1 Navigation

Date: 2026-06-13  
Status: Accepted  
Scope: V1

## Context

TEMIS should feel technical, network-aware, and exploratory from the first version. It still needs to stay simple enough for editors to manage manually.

## Decision

V1 navigation will use editorially managed topic relationships.

Topic relationships are simple unlabeled links between topics. They are used to help readers move between related themes and content.

## Consequences

- Editors can shape the navigation experience without complex modeling.
- The homepage and topic pages can expose a relationship-based browsing pattern.
- Topic relationships stay simple and manually edited for V1.
- Topic visibility and priority controls may be used to prevent visual clutter.
