# ADR-0006: Keep V1 Publishing Team-Managed

Date: 2026-06-13  
Status: Accepted  
Scope: V1

## Context

V1 should launch as a curated editorial website. The first content will be created and managed by the team.

## Decision

TEMIS V1 will not include public contribution intake, public reader accounts, or community publishing workflows.

All V1 public content is created, edited, and published by authorized team members in the CMS.

## Consequences

- The implementation can avoid public account management and contribution intake flows.
- Editorial quality control stays with the team for launch.
- Public readers can focus on discovering, reading, subscribing, and sharing.
- If public contribution flows are needed later, they should be designed and approved separately.
